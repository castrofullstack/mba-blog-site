title: "Designing Better Business Writing"
description: "How to make business writing clearer, more useful, and easier for busy readers to act on."
pubDate: 2026-04-28
tags: ["Writing", "Communication"]
---

The best business writing respects the reader's attention. It starts with the point, explains the stakes, and makes the next action easy to find.

Before polishing a draft, ask:

- What should the reader understand after one minute?
- What decision, action, or change should this writing support?
- Which details are useful evidence, and which details are only proof of effort?

Clear writing is generous. It lowers the cost of thinking for everyone who has to use it.
